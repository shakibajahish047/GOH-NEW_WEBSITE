Supporting communities in need across Afghanistan

Our Work in Food & Clothes Distribution

Gate of Hope is dedicated to providing essential food and clothing to vulnerable families and communities in Afghanistan. We believe no one should go hungry or lack basic necessities. Our programs focus on distributing food packages, winter clothing, and emergency relief materials to those most in need.

By delivering food and clothes, we aim to improve living conditions, promote dignity, and help families overcome hardships.

Winter Clothes Distribution

Year: 2023

Goal: Provide warm clothing to 1,500 families during winter.

This project provided winter clothes including coats, jackets, and blankets to families in need, helping them stay warm during harsh winter months.

Emergency Food Packages

Year: 2023

Goal: Deliver food aid to 2,000 households in crisis areas.

We distributed essential food supplies such as rice, flour, oil, and lentils to families facing food insecurity.

Community Clothing Drives

Year: 2022

Goal: Collect and distribute gently used clothes to 1,000 children and adults.

This initiative collected donations of used clothes from communities and redistributed them to families and individuals in need.
