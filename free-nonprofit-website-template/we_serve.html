Whom We Serve

At Gate Of Hope, our programs are designed to serve the most vulnerable in Afghanistan. We focus on empowering individuals and communities through education, livelihood support, protection, and humanitarian assistance.

Returnees & IDPs

  • Protection & Psychosocial Support
  • Emergency Aid (Food, Shelter, NFIs)
  • Livelihood Training

Women (Widows, Survivors, Vulnerable)

  • Legal Awareness & Support
  • Vocational & Skills Training
  • Emergency Support Programs

Children

  • Access to Education
  • Psychosocial Support
  • Food & Nutrition Programs

Elderly & Disabled

  • Health Support
  • Community Care Programs
  • Emergency Assistance
